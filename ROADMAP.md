## Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Computational intractability**: Develop approximation algorithms early
- **Limited real-world applicability**: Prioritize empirical validation
- **Parameter sensitivity**: Extensive sensitivity analysis

### Resource Risks
- **Limited development time**: Focus on core functionality first
- **Insufficient expertise**: Engage domain experts for validation
- **Funding constraints**: Seek grants and academic partnerships

## Dependencies

### Internal
- Completion of mathematical proofs
- Algorithm development expertise
- Access to validation datasets

### External
- Academic review process timelines
- Availability of domain experts
- Computing resources for large-scale testing
